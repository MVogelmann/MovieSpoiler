Split

Released: 01/2017

Starring

  • James McAvoy as the kidnapper
  • Anya Taylor-Joy as Casey Cooke
  • Betty Buckley as Dr. Karen Fletcher
  • Haley Lu Richardson as Claire Benoit

Movie Quote

Kevin: The broken are the more evolved.

Plot

After a birthday party three girls, around 14 years old, get kidnapped by a man who has 23 different personalities. At the beginning, the kidnapper is "Dennis", but he changes his personalities to e.g. "Kevin", who is in therapy with Dr. Fletcher, or "Hedwig", a nine year old boy. The situation lets the three girls grow more and more together. Dr. Fletcher gets alarmed by the number of split personalities of the man and decides to visit his place at the middle of the night. She secretly finds the three kidnapped girls, but the split man recognizes it and he changes into the 24th personality, called the beast. He kills Dr. Fletcher and two of the girls, but one girl can escape from him.
